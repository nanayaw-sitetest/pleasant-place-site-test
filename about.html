THE MANDATE

Pleasant Place Church International was not birthed out of a desire for another church, but out of a divine burden to restore the Standard of Excellence to the Body of Christ.

For over two decades, we have served as a training ground for visionaries. Our foundation is built on the belief that a child of God should be the most efficient, most creative, and most influential person in any room. We don't just preach the Gospel; we demonstrate the Gospel through lifestyle, leadership, and legacy.

From our humble beginnings, Dr. Bishop Gideon Titi-Ofei saw a place where "Pleasantness" met "Power"—a sanctuary where the presence of God would refine the character of men into the likeness of kings. Today, that vision spans continents, touching lives through our media missions, community outreaches, and kingdom-driven leadership summits.
